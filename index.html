About Sam

Where Artistry Meets Confidence

The Ways of Sam is a premium makeup and hairstyling brand based in Mansfield, Nottingham. Founded by Sam — a passionate, detail-driven beauty artist — this brand was built on one belief: every client deserves to feel extraordinary.

From intimate birthday glam sessions to breathtaking bridal transformations, each appointment is a curated experience. Sam brings not just skill and professionalism, but warmth and genuine care to every client he works with.

Tailored to You — No two clients are the same. Every look is created to complement your unique features and personal style.
Premium Products Only — High-end, skin-loving products that photograph beautifully and last all day.
Client-First Always — A relaxed, welcoming atmosphere where you feel heard, pampered and confident.
Trusted by Hundreds — From weddings to photoshoots, Sam's work speaks for itself across Mansfield and Nottingham.

Questions? We Have Answers

Everything you need to know before booking your session.

For weddings, we recommend booking at least 3–6 months in advance, especially during peak season (May–September). For events, parties, and photoshoots, 2–4 weeks is usually sufficient — though earlier is always better to secure your preferred date.

Yes! Sam is mobile and can come to your home, venue, or chosen location across Mansfield, Nottingham, and surrounding areas. Travel fees may apply for locations further afield — this will be discussed when booking.

Absolutely. A bridal trial is strongly recommended and can be booked separately. It's a chance to test the look, discuss your preferences, and ensure everything is perfect before your wedding day. Trials are typically held 4–8 weeks before the big day.

Sam uses only premium, professional-grade products from trusted brands. The products are skin-friendly, long-lasting, and suitable for all skin tones and types. If you have any known allergies or sensitivities, please let Sam know in advance.

Yes, a deposit is required to secure your booking. The deposit amount varies depending on the service and will be confirmed at time of booking. Deposits are non-refundable but may be transferred to a new date with sufficient notice.

Session times vary by service. Traditional makeup typically takes 60–90 minutes. Bridal makeup and hair together can take 2–3 hours. Photoshoot sessions depend on the number of looks required. Exact timings will be discussed at booking.
